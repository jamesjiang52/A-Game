There are nominally six different combat options:
Powerful Strike
Lithe Probe
Attempt Parry
Dodge
Feint 
False Retreat

With each dammage attempt there are three possible results:
Missed - Deals 0% of damage		15% of the time
Normal - Deals 100% of damage		70% of the time
Critical - Deals 200% of damage		15% of the time
(When the possibilities are changed, they are added/deducted from the normal)


Powerful Strike(+10% critical, +5% missed):
 - Signifiant Victory: Attempt Parry (Deal Damage + Opponent Stagger)
 - Slight Victory: Feint (Deal Damage)
 - Slight Defeat: False Retreat (Stagger)
 - Significant Defeat: Dodge (Significant Stagger)
 - Draws: Lithe probe (Deal Damage + Take Damage)

Lithe Probe(-10% missed, -5% critical):
 - Significant Victory: Dodge (Deal Significant Damage)
 - Slight Victory: Feint (Deal Damage)
 - Slight Defeat: False Retreat (+10% opponenet critical)
 - Significant Defeat: Attempt Parry (Stagger + 50% damage taken increase)
 - Draws: Powerful Strike (Deal Damage + Take Damage)

Attempt Parry(-5% critical):
 - Significant Victory: Lithe Probe (Opponent Stagger + 50% damage taken increase)
 - Significant Victory: False Retreat (Significant Opponent Stagger)
 - Significant Defeat: Feint (Stagger + -10% opponent missed)
 - Significant Defeat: Powerful Strike (Take Damage + Stagger)
 - Draws: Dodge (Stagger + Opponent Stagger)

Dodge(+5% missed):
 - Significant Victory: Powerful Strike (Significant Opponent Stagger)
 - Significant Victory: False Retreat (Significant Opponent Stagger)
 - Significant Defeat: Feint (Stagger + +10% oponent critical)
 - Significant Defeat: Lithe Probe (Take Significant Damage)
 - Draws: Attempt Parry (Stagger + Opponent Stagger)

Feint(+5% opponent stagger):
 - Significant Victory: Attempt Parry (Opponent Stagger + -10% missed)
 - Significant Victory: Dodge (Opponent Stagger + +10% critical)
 - Slight Defeat: Powerful Strike (Take Damage)
 - Slight Defeat: Lithe Probe (Take Damage)
 - Slight Defeat: False Retreat (Stagger)

False Retreat(-5% stagger):
 - Slight Victory: Powerful Strike (Opponent Stagger)
 - Slight Victory: Lithe Proble (Opponent Stagger)
 - Slight Victory: Feint (Opponenet Stagger)
 - Significant Defeat: Attempt Parry (Significant Stagger)
 - Significant Defeat: Dodge (Significant Stagger)

With the usage of a shield, to more options are unlocked:
Block
Bash

Block: 
 - Significant Victory: Powerful Strike (Opponenet Stagger + Chance opponent has to change weapon)
 - Significant Victory: Lithe Probe (Significant Opponent Stagger)
 - Slight Victory: False Retreat (Opponenet Stagger)
 - Slight Defeat: Feint (Stagger)
 - Slight Defeat: Dodge (Stagger)
 - Slight Defeat: Attempt Parry (Stagger)
 - Draws: Bash (Both shields are broken and cannot be used for the rest of the fight)

Bash:
 - Significant Victory: Attempt Parry (Opponent Stagger + Chance opponent has to change weapon)
 - Significant Victory: Dodge (Damage + Stagger)
 - Slight Victory: Feint (Deal Damage)
 - Slight Defeat: False Retreat (Stagger)
 - Slight Defeat: Powerful Strike (Take Damage)
 - Slight Defeat: Lithe Probe (Take Damage)
 - Draws: Block (Both shields are broken and cannot be used for the rest of the fight)

All effects are temporary and lasts for the next turn
